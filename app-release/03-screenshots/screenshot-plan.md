# Dengo Screenshot Plan - Complete Storyboard

## Screenshot Strategy Overview

### Primary Goal
To showcase the emotional connection and daily ritual aspects of Dengo while demonstrating its core functionality in an appealing, lifestyle-focused manner.

### Target Audience
- Couples aged 25-45
- Looking for connection in busy lives
- Value emotional intimacy and communication
- Seek sustainable, light-touch solutions

### Visual Guidelines
- **Color Palette:** Dengo's warm gradient (chama sorridente reference)
- **Tone:** Warm, inviting, authentic
- **Focus:** Emotional connection and ease of use
- **Style:** Clean, uncluttered, lifestyle-oriented

## Visual Design Guidelines

### Consistency Elements
- Maintain Dengo's warm gradient theme throughout
- Use consistent typography and spacing
- Ensure all screenshots have the same visual treatment
- Apply subtle shadows and depth effects uniformly

### Readability Requirements
- Text overlays should be 24pt minimum
- High contrast between text and background
- Avoid text being obscured by UI elements
- Ensure text is readable on all device sizes

### Lifestyle Integration
- Show screenshots in realistic usage contexts
- Use warm, intimate lighting where appropriate
- Avoid sterile or overly clinical imagery
- Focus on emotional connection and positive feelings

## Copy Guidelines

### Language Tone
- Warm and inviting
- Benefit-focused rather than feature-focused
- Emotionally resonant
- Clear and concise

### Localization Notes
- Portuguese copy should feel natural and culturally appropriate
- English copy should be accessible to US/UK audiences
- Avoid idioms that don't translate well
- Consider cultural differences in relationship dynamics
