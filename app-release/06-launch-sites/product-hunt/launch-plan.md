# Product Hunt Launch Plan - Dengo

## Pre-Launch Preparation (2-3 Weeks Before)

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Finalize all Product Hunt listing content (PT/EN)
- [ ] Prepare all required and recommended assets
- [ ] Create FAQ responses and engagement materials
- [ ] Identify and prepare influencer/early adopter contacts
- [ ] Set up tracking and analytics for launch performance
- [ ] Prepare backup content and responses

### Week 2: Community Building
- [ ] Engage with Product Hunt community in related topics
- [ ] Share preliminary content and build anticipation
- [ ] Connect with other makers in the relationship/wellness space
- [ ] Prepare launch announcement for email list (if available)
- [ ] Coordinate with any launch partners or supporters
- [ ] Finalize all technical requirements and compliance

### Week 3: Final Preparations
- [ ] Complete all assets and run final quality checks
- [ ] Prepare launch day schedule and responsibilities
- [ ] Confirm all team members' availability for launch day
- [ ] Prepare templates for responding to common questions
- [ ] Test all links and functionality
- [ ] Final review of legal compliance and app store readiness

## Launch Day Timeline

### 2 Hours Before Launch
- [ ] Final verification of all assets and content
- [ ] Team brief on launch strategy and responsibilities
- [ ] Confirm all systems and tracking are working
- [ ] Prepare for immediate engagement (have responses ready)

### Launch Hour (T+0)
- [ ] Submit Product Hunt listing
- [ ] Post initial maker update with compelling story
- [ ] Share on all social media channels
- [ ] Send to email list if available
- [ ] Begin active engagement with early commenters
- [ ] Monitor performance metrics in real-time

### Hours 1-6 Post-Launch
- [ ] Respond to all comments within 30 minutes
- [ ] Share in relevant communities and groups
- [ ] Engage with upvoters and early supporters
- [ ] Monitor trending status and adjust strategy
- [ ] Prepare follow-up content and updates
- [ ] Track engagement metrics and sentiment

### Hours 6-24 Post-Launch
- [ ] Continue active engagement with community
- [ ] Post follow-up updates with additional value
- [ ] Share user-generated content and testimonials
- [ ] Monitor for press or media interest
- [ ] Prepare responses to common questions
- [ ] Track download/conversion metrics

## Engagement Strategy

### Maker Update Strategy
- Share authentic story behind the app
- Explain the problem being solved
- Highlight unique value proposition
- Invite feedback and questions
- Show personality and passion
- Connect on human level

### Response Strategy
- Respond within 30 minutes during first 6 hours
- Address questions with detailed, helpful answers
- Acknowledge feedback and suggestions
- Show appreciation for support
- Guide conversation toward value proposition
- Maintain brand voice and tone

### Content Strategy
- Share additional context about features
- Provide examples of use cases
- Share research or inspiration behind app
- Offer tips for getting most from app
- Address common concerns or hesitations
- Highlight user testimonials (if available)

## Community Outreach

### Target Communities
- [ ] Relationship and communication forums
- [ ] Wellness and mental health communities
- [ ] Product and startup communities
- [ ] Mobile app user groups
- [ ] Couples-focused social media groups
- [ ] Personal development communities

### Outreach Approach
- [ ] Personalized messages to relevant community members
- [ ] Offer value before asking for support
- [ ] Share behind-the-scenes content
- [ ] Provide exclusive early access (if appropriate)
- [ ] Engage genuinely with community discussions
- [ ] Follow up without being pushy

## Success Metrics and Tracking

### Primary Metrics
- [ ] Product Hunt rank and trending status
- [ ] Upvotes and comments received
- [ ] Social media shares and mentions
- [ ] App downloads/installation rate
- [ ] Day 1/Day 7 retention rate
- [ ] Press and media coverage received

### Secondary Metrics
- [ ] Email signups (if applicable)
- [ ] Social media follower growth
- [ ] Quality of comments and feedback
- [ ] User-generated content created
- [ ] Influencer mentions or shares
- [ ] Long-term retention impact

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks
- [ ] Low initial engagement
- [ ] Negative feedback or criticism
- [ ] Technical issues with app during launch
- [ ] Competitor launches on same day
- [ ] Team unavailability during critical hours
- [ ] Compliance or policy issues

### Mitigation Strategies
- [ ] Prepare backup engagement plan if initial response is low
- [ ] Train team on handling negative feedback professionally
- [ ] Ensure app stability and scalability before launch
- [ ] Monitor competitor activity and adjust timing if needed
- [ ] Have backup team members available
- [ ] Review all policies and compliance requirements

## Post-Launch Activities

### Immediate (24-48 Hours)
- [ ] Thank all supporters personally
- [ ] Address any outstanding questions
- [ ] Share launch results and gratitude
- [ ] Begin follow-up with interested parties
- [ ] Document lessons learned
- [ ] Plan next steps based on feedback

### Short-term (1 Week)
- [ ] Analyze performance metrics
- [ ] Follow up with media contacts
- [ ] Engage with new users from launch
- [ ] Prepare content based on feedback received
- [ ] Plan next phase of growth
- [ ] Update roadmap based on feedback

### Long-term (1 Month)
- [ ] Evaluate overall launch success
- [ ] Measure impact on user acquisition
- [ ] Assess quality of new users
- [ ] Plan improvements based on feedback
- [ ] Prepare for next milestone
- [ ] Document launch learnings for future use

- [ ] Communication with external parties

- [ ] Social media management during launch
- [ ] Monitoring sentiment and feedback

- [ ] Monitor performance metrics
