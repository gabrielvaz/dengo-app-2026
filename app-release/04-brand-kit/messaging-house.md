# Messaging House - Dengo

## Brand Foundation

### Mission Statement
Dengo transforma o cuidado com relacionamentos em um hábito leve e prazeroso, fortalecendo a conexão emocional entre casais com flashcards diários significativos.

### Vision Statement
Um mundo onde todos os casais tenham rituais diários de conexão que os mantenham emocionalmente próximos, mesmo com vidas corridas.

### Values
1. **Conexão Autêntica** - Promovemos interações reais e significativas
2. **Respeito à Intimidade** - Honoramos o espaço sagrado das relações
3. **Acessibilidade Emocional** - Tornamos a conexão disponível para todos
4. **Leveza Intencional** - Práticas sustentáveis e não pressionantes
5. **Crescimento Mútuo** - Apoiamos o desenvolvimento conjunto

## Core Messaging Pillars

### Pillar 1: Daily Ritual of Connection
**Core Message:** Transform emotional connection into a sustainable daily habit
**Supporting Messages:**
- Just 5 minutes a day can strengthen your relationship
- Consistent small actions create lasting connection
- Daily practice builds emotional intimacy over time
- Ritual creates predictability and safety in relationships

**Proof Points:**
- Curated flashcards for daily use
- Personalized to relationship stage
- Offline-first approach for convenience
- Progress tracking to maintain consistency

**Target Audience:** Couples seeking sustainable connection practices

### Pillar 2: Meaningful Content Curation
**Core Message:** Expertly crafted questions that stimulate real conversations
**Supporting Messages:**
- Questions that wouldn't happen spontaneously
- Curated by relationship experts
- Adapted to relationship stage and interests
- Balanced between light and deep topics

**Proof Points:**
- Hundreds of carefully designed flashcards
- Multiple categories (Casais, Almas Gêmeas, etc.)
- Sensitivity levels for different comfort zones
- Regular content updates

**Target Audience:** Couples wanting to deepen their conversations

### Pillar 3: Privacy-First Approach
**Core Message:** Your intimate moments stay between you and your partner
**Supporting Messages:**
- Content available offline
- No data sharing without consent
- Secure and private experience
- Respect for relationship boundaries

**Proof Points:**
- Offline functionality
- Minimal data collection
- Clear privacy policy
- Local data storage options

**Target Audience:** Privacy-conscious couples

## Competitive Differentiation

### vs. Generic Relationship Apps
**Dengo:** Focus on daily rituals and sustainable practices
**Others:** Often therapy-focused or problem-solving oriented
**Message:** "Não é terapia, é um ritual diário de cuidado emocional"

### vs. Communication Tools
**Dengo:** Emphasis on light, enjoyable connection
**Others:** Often task-oriented or structured communication
**Message:** "Simples o suficiente para ser sustentável, mas profundo o bastante para gerar impacto real"

### vs. Dating Apps
**Dengo:** For established relationships, not new connections
**Others:** For finding new partners
**Message:** "Para casais apaixonados que querem manter a conexão emocional"

## Messaging Consistency Guidelines

### Must Always Include
- Connection-focused language
- Emphasis on daily/sustainable practice
- Privacy and safety reassurance
- Positive, growth-oriented framing

### Should Never Include
- Problem-focused language
- Clinical or therapeutic terminology
- Pressure-inducing messaging
- Gender-specific assumptions

### Regular Review Process
- Monthly messaging audit
- Quarterly competitive analysis
- Bi-annual audience research review
- Annual messaging house refresh

## Crisis Messaging Framework

### Potential Issues and Responses
**Content Sensitivity Concerns:**
- "All content is designed with consent and mutual respect as priorities"
- "We maintain strict editorial guidelines for appropriate content"

**Privacy Concerns:**
- "We collect minimal data and prioritize your privacy"
- "Offline functionality ensures your intimate moments stay private"

**Relationship Stress:**
- "Dengo is about connection, not fixing problems"
- "We create a safe space for exploration at your own pace"
