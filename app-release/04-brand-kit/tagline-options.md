# Tagline Options - Dengo

## Primary Tagline Options

### Option 1: Connection Focus
**Portuguese:** O ritual diário de conexão que seu relacionamento merece
**English:** The daily connection ritual your relationship deserves

**Rationale:** Emphasizes both the daily practice and the deserving nature of the relationship, positioning Dengo as something special and valuable.

### Option 2: Simplicity Focus
**Portuguese:** Conexão diária para casais modernos
**English:** Daily connection for modern couples

**Rationale:** Direct and clear, communicates the core benefit (daily connection) and target audience (modern couples).

### Option 3: Transformation Focus
**Portuguese:** Transforme o cuidado com sua relação em um hábito leve
**English:** Transform caring for your relationship into a light habit

**Rationale:** Focuses on the transformation from potentially difficult relationship care to an easy, light habit.

### Option 4: Question Focus
**Portuguese:** Perguntas diárias para fortalecer sua relação
**English:** Daily questions to strengthen your relationship

**Rationale:** Highlights the core mechanism (questions) and benefit (strengthens relationship) in a clear, functional way.

## Secondary Tagline Options

### Option 5: Ritual Focus
**Portuguese:** O ritual diário de conexão emocional
**English:** The daily ritual of emotional connection

**Rationale:** Emphasizes the ritualistic nature of the practice, appealing to those who value routine and intentionality.

### Option 6: Growth Focus
**Portuguese:** Cresçam juntos com perguntas que conectam
**English:** Grow together with connecting questions

**Rationale:** Appeals to couples focused on growth and development, emphasizing the connecting power of the questions.

### Option 7: Lightness Focus
**Portuguese:** Leveza e conexão para o seu relacionamento
**English:** Lightness and connection for your relationship

**Rationale:** Balances the "light" approach with the core benefit of connection, appealing to those who want something undemanding.

### Option 8: Discovery Focus
**Portuguese:** Descubra seu parceiro com perguntas que aproximam
**English:** Discover your partner with questions that bring you closer

**Rationale:** Appeals to the curiosity and discovery aspects, emphasizing the "getting closer" benefit.

## Tagline Testing Framework

### A/B Testing Considerations
1. **Connection vs. Question Focus:** Test emotional benefit vs. functional mechanism
2. **Formal vs. Casual Tone:** Test more formal "relação" vs. casual "casal"
3. **Benefit vs. Behavior:** Test outcome-focused vs. action-focused messaging
4. **Length Variations:** Test short punchy vs. more descriptive taglines

### Performance Metrics
- Click-through rates on ads with different taglines
- App store conversion rates
- Social media engagement rates
- Brand recall and recognition tests
- User preference surveys

## Tagline Implementation Guidelines

- **Social Media:** Use the memorable, shareable taglines
- **Marketing Materials:** Use the emotionally resonant taglines
- **Internal Communications:** Use the brand-consistent primary tagline
- **Partnerships:** Adapt based on partner audience and context

### Localization Considerations
- Maintain emotional resonance in translation
- Consider cultural differences in relationship terminology
- Test local preference for formal vs. informal language
- Ensure taglines work in both Brazilian Portuguese and European Portuguese if needed
