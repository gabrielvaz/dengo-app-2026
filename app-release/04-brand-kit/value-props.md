# Value Propositions - Dengo

## Primary Value Proposition

### Main Value Proposition
**English:** The daily ritual of emotional connection your relationship deserves
**Portuguese:** O ritual diário de conexão emocional que seu relacionamento merece

**Supporting Statement:** Dengo transforms caring for your relationship into a light and enjoyable habit with meaningful daily flashcards, helping loving couples maintain emotional connection even with busy schedules.

## Customer Value Propositions

**Value Proposition:** Sustainable daily practice for emotional connection
**Key Benefits:**
- Just 5 minutes per day commitment
- No special skills or training required
- Works with busy schedules
- Builds intimacy gradually and naturally
- Creates positive relationship rituals

**Pain Points Solved:**
- Lack of time for meaningful conversations
- Difficulty starting deep conversations
- Routine and complacency in relationships
- Not knowing what to talk about
- Feeling disconnected despite being together

## Unique Value Propositions

### Differentiation 1: Daily Ritual Approach
**What:** Focus on sustainable daily practice rather than intensive sessions
**Why It Matters:** Creates lasting habits that integrate into daily life
**Evidence:** 5-minute daily commitment vs. hour-long sessions
**Competitive Advantage:** More sustainable than therapy apps or intensive programs

### Differentiation 2: Curated Content Quality
**What:** Expertly crafted questions organized by categories and relationship stages
**Why It Matters:** Eliminates the guesswork of what to talk about
**Evidence:** Hundreds of carefully designed flashcards across 12+ categories
**Competitive Advantage:** More thoughtful than generic question lists or chatbots

### Differentiation 3: Privacy-First Design
**What:** Offline functionality with minimal data collection
**Why It Matters:** Protects intimate relationship moments
**Evidence:** Works without internet, local data storage
**Competitive Advantage:** More private than social connection apps or platforms

### Differentiation 4: Emotional Safety
**What:** Gentle, non-pressured approach to intimacy
**Why It Matters:** Creates safe space for vulnerable conversations
**Evidence:** Sensitivity levels, consent-focused content, optional sharing
**Competitive Advantage:** More comfortable than therapy apps or clinical approaches
