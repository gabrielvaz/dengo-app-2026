# Risk Mitigation Plan - Dengo

## High-Priority Risks

### 1. App Store Rejection
**Risk:** App is rejected during review process, delaying launch

**Impact:** High - Could delay launch by 1-4 weeks
**Probability:** Medium - 20% chance of initial rejection

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Submit app 2 weeks before planned launch date
- Conduct thorough self-review using store guidelines
- Use external reviewer for fresh perspective
- Prepare alternative launch dates
- Have legal team review content for compliance
- Prepare appeal documentation in advance

**Contingency Plans:**
- Immediate: Activate backup launch timeline
- Short-term: Engage with app store support team
- Long-term: Prepare revised app for resubmission

**Monitoring:**
- Track app store communication daily
- Monitor review process timeline
- Prepare team for quick response to requests

### 2. Technical Failures During Launch
**Risk:** App crashes, server overload, or critical bugs during high-traffic launch

**Impact:** High - Could severely damage initial user experience
**Probability:** Medium - 15% chance of significant technical issues

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Conduct extensive load testing before launch
- Implement comprehensive monitoring and alerting
- Prepare staging environment for quick fixes
- Ensure 24/7 technical support availability
- Create rollback procedures for quick recovery
- Test all third-party integrations thoroughly

**Contingency Plans:**
- Immediate: Activate monitoring and incident response
- Short-term: Deploy hotfix or rollback if necessary
- Long-term: Implement additional stability measures

**Monitoring:**
- Real-time performance metrics
- Error rate tracking
- User session monitoring
- Server resource utilization

### 3. Negative User Reception
**Risk:** Poor initial reviews, low ratings, or negative social media feedback

**Impact:** High - Could affect long-term adoption and credibility
**Probability:** Medium - 25% chance of negative initial feedback

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Conduct beta testing with diverse user groups
- Prepare excellent customer support response team
- Create positive user onboarding experience
- Implement in-app feedback collection
- Prepare rapid response protocols for negative feedback
- Train team on empathetic communication

**Contingency Plans:**
- Immediate: Engage with negative reviewers professionally
- Short-term: Address issues raised in feedback
- Long-term: Implement improvements based on feedback

**Monitoring:**
- App store review tracking
- Social media sentiment analysis
- User feedback aggregation
- Support ticket analysis

## Medium-Priority Risks

### 4. Insufficient Marketing Reach
**Risk:** Lower than expected awareness and initial downloads

**Impact:** Medium - Could slow growth and market penetration
**Probability:** Medium - 30% chance of underperforming expectations

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Develop multiple marketing channels and tactics
- Create backup marketing campaigns
- Build relationships with influencers before launch
- Prepare paid advertising budget for boost
- Leverage existing networks and communities
- Create viral content opportunities

**Contingency Plans:**
- Immediate: Activate backup marketing channels
- Short-term: Increase paid advertising spend
- Long-term: Refine target audience and messaging

**Monitoring:**
- Daily download metrics
- Marketing channel performance
- Cost per acquisition tracking
- Viral coefficient measurement

### 5. Content Moderation Issues
**Risk:** Controversial or inappropriate content leading to negative publicity

**Impact:** Medium - Could damage brand reputation
**Probability:** Low - 10% chance of content-related controversy

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Implement content review process before publishing
- Create clear content guidelines and standards
- Establish user reporting mechanisms
- Prepare crisis communication protocols
- Regular content audit and review
- Legal review of sensitive content categories

**Contingency Plans:**
- Immediate: Remove problematic content immediately
- Short-term: Communicate transparently about actions taken
- Long-term: Strengthen content moderation processes

**Monitoring:**
- User content reports tracking
- Social media mentions monitoring
- Content quality audits
- Legal compliance checks

### 6. Privacy and Data Security Concerns
**Risk:** Data breach, privacy concerns, or regulatory compliance issues

**Impact:** High - Could result in legal issues and reputation damage
**Probability:** Low - 5% chance of significant privacy incident

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Implement robust security measures and encryption
- Conduct security audits before launch
- Create clear privacy policy and data handling procedures
- Limit data collection to essential information only
- Prepare incident response team and procedures
- Ensure compliance with data protection regulations

**Contingency Plans:**
- Immediate: Activate security incident response protocol
- Short-term: Communicate transparently with users
- Long-term: Implement additional security measures

**Monitoring:**
- Security monitoring and alerting
- Privacy compliance audits
- Data access logging
- Security vulnerability scanning

## Low-Priority Risks

### 7. Competitor Response
**Risk:** Competitors launch competing features or aggressive marketing campaigns

**Impact:** Medium - Could affect market share and positioning
**Probability:** Medium - 20% chance of competitive response

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Monitor competitor activity regularly
- Maintain unique value proposition
- Build strong user loyalty and engagement
- Prepare rapid feature development capabilities
- Create strong brand identity and recognition
- Develop competitive intelligence capabilities

**Contingency Plans:**
- Immediate: Assess competitor impact on our metrics
- Short-term: Adjust positioning or marketing if needed
- Long-term: Develop differentiation strategies

**Monitoring:**
- Competitor app updates tracking
- Market positioning analysis
- Share of voice monitoring
- Competitive feature analysis

### 8. Team Capacity Issues
**Risk:** Team unable to handle launch demands or unexpected issues

**Impact:** Medium - Could affect response time and quality
**Probability:** Low - 10% chance of resource constraints

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Plan team capacity with buffer for launch period
- Prepare external support resources if needed
- Create detailed process documentation
- Cross-train team members on critical functions
- Schedule team availability during launch period
- Prepare remote work capabilities

**Contingency Plans:**
- Immediate: Activate backup team members or external support
- Short-term: Adjust priorities and timelines
- Long-term: Improve team capacity planning

**Monitoring:**
- Team workload tracking
- Response time monitoring
- Burnout indicators assessment
- Resource allocation efficiency
