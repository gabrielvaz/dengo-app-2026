## Critical Path Dependencies

### Store Approval Process
- App Store review: 24-48 hours (can extend to 1+ weeks)
- Play Store review: 1-3 days (can extend for new apps)
- **Risk:** Delays can shift entire launch timeline
- **Mitigation:** Submit early, have backup dates

### Marketing Content Approval
- Legal review of all marketing materials
- Cultural appropriateness validation
- **Risk:** Content changes needed late in process
- **Mitigation:** Early review process, backup content

### Technical Readiness
- App stability and performance validation
- Server capacity for launch traffic
- **Risk:** Technical issues during launch
- **Mitigation:** Comprehensive testing, monitoring tools

## Timeline Contingencies

### 1-Week Delay Scenario
- Push all marketing content by 1 week
- Adjust social media calendar
- Update all stakeholder communications
- Maintain momentum with continued teasers

### 2-Week Delay Scenario
- Reassess market timing and competition
- Refresh marketing content to remain relevant
- Update influencer and media contacts
- Consider new promotional opportunities

### Critical Delay Scenario (>2 weeks)
- Rebuild entire timeline from new launch date
- Reassess market conditions and competition
- Update all marketing and PR strategies
- Evaluate if launch date should be reconsidered
