# Data Safety - Play Store (Dengo)

## Data Safety Form Structure and Responses

### Data Types Collected
**Does your app collect, share, or sell user data?** NO

- Personal and sensitive information: NO
- Messages and communications: NO
- Photos and videos: NO
- Audio information: NO
- App activity: NO
- Device or other IDs: NO
- Purchase history: NO
- Crash reports: NO
- Diagnostics: NO
- Other usage information: NO

### On-device Data (Not Collected by Developer)
The app stores user preferences and progress **locally on the device** to personalize the experience. This data is **not transmitted off-device**.

### Data Sharing and Selling
- Data sharing: NO
- Data selling: NO

### Data Security Practices
Because the app does not collect data, there is no transmission or server-side storage. Local data remains on the user's device.

### Data Deletion
Users can delete all local data via **Profile > Apagar todos os dados e resetar app**, or by uninstalling the app.

### Compliance Statement
Dengo does not collect or share user data. All data stays on the device and is controlled by the user.
