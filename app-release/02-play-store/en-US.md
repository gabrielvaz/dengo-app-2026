### Main Description
Dengo is the daily ritual of emotional connection your relationship deserves. With light, fun, and meaningful flashcards, we help loving couples keep the spark alive, even with demanding schedules.

### Features
• **Real Connection:** Carefully crafted questions to stimulate conversations that wouldn't happen spontaneously
• **Daily Ritual:** A sustainable 5-minute habit to strengthen your connection
• **Curated Content:** Hundreds of flashcards organized by categories (Couples, Soulmates, Deep Questions, Would You Rather, etc.)
• **Personalization:** Adapted to your relationship stage and couple's interests
• **Offline First:** Works without internet, protecting your privacy

### How It Works
1. Answer the quick profile questionnaire
2. Receive personalized daily flashcards
3. Share with your partner and talk about what really matters
4. Develop emotional intimacy with light and meaningful rituals

### Content Categories

• Deep Questions - Emotional intimacy

• Family Mode - For couples with children
